# Theme 3: Fantasy / Medieval Castle

**Aesthetic:** Stone, wood, and magic. Every infrastructure component is reimagined as a medieval castle feature or magical artifact. Architecture decisions become kingdom-building decisions. Spells and enchantments serve as the tech layer.

**Palette:** Cool stone grays, warm wood browns, royal purple/blue, gold trim, cyan magical glow for enchantments (health layer).

**Shared traits:** Each component sits on a cobblestone or flagstone base. Magical runes or glowing enchantments provide the cyan highlight layer. Mix of practical medieval structures and arcane artifacts.

---

## Components

### Server
A wizard's tower — small stone turret with a pointed roof and a glowing cyan crystal at the top. An arched window shows a busy scribe inside. Magical energy crackles around the spire.

### Database
A treasure vault — reinforced stone chamber with an iron-banded oak door. Gold coins and scrolls visible through a barred window. A magical seal (cyan rune) locks the door.

### Data Cache
A crystal ball on an ornate pedestal — the ball glows cyan and shows swirling images. Smaller and faster than consulting the vault. Draped purple velvet on the stand.

### Load Balancer
A crossroads signpost — tall wooden post with multiple directional arrow signs pointing different ways. A magical fairy/wisp (cyan) orbits the post, directing travelers.

### CDN
A messenger hawk perch — wooden tower with a trained hawk sitting on top. A small scroll case is strapped to the hawk's leg. Cyan wind trails show its flight range.

### API Gateway
A castle gatehouse — stone archway with a raised portcullis, two small guard towers flanking it. A shield with a keyhole emblem hangs above. Guards check credentials.

### Queue
A line of adventurers at a quest board — a wooden notice board with parchment postings. Small figures wait in an orderly queue. Cyan magical rope barriers keep the line.

### Worker
A blacksmith's forge — stone hearth with bellows, an anvil, and a hammer mid-strike. Cyan magical sparks fly from the metal being worked. A bucket of finished swords beside it.

### Streaming Server
A bard's stage — small wooden platform with a lute-playing bard. Musical notes (cyan) float outward in a continuous stream. An enchanted megaphone amplifies the performance.

### Edge Cache
A roadside supply cache — a stone waypoint marker with a small chest at its base. Travelers can grab supplies without returning to the castle. Cyan protection rune on the chest.

### DNS/GTM
An enchanted map table — a large parchment map spread on a stone table with magical pins that move on their own. Cyan ley lines connect locations. A compass rose glows in the corner.

### Blob Storage
A dragon's hoard cave — a small cave entrance with gold, gems, and oversized artifacts spilling out. Much larger capacity than the vault. A sleeping dragon eye (cyan) peeks from the darkness.

### Circuit Breaker
A magical ward stone — a standing stone monolith with carved runes. When danger is detected, a cyan force field dome activates around it. Cracks appear when under stress.
