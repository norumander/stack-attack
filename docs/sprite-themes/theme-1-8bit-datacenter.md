# Theme 1: 8-Bit Office / Data Center

**Aesthetic:** Chunky pixel art reminiscent of early office simulators and retro tech. Think server rooms with blinking lights, tangled cables, and humming equipment — all rendered in a warm, nostalgic low-res style. Matches the Pico-8 landing page vibe.

**Palette:** Warm grays, beige/cream (old plastic casings), green/amber status LEDs, soft blue indicator lights. Pico-8 compatible palette preferred.

**Shared traits:** Every component is a recognizable piece of office/datacenter hardware sitting on a small floor tile. Blocky, approachable, no sharp edges. Status LEDs or indicator lights serve as the cyan health-highlight layer.

---

## Components

### Server
Beige tower PC / rackmount unit with a chunky power button and blinking green LED strip on the front panel. Visible fan grille on the side. Thick power cable trailing off the back.

### Database
Stack of 3 chunky hard drives / disk platters in a beige enclosure. LED activity lights on the front blink in sequence. A small label reads "DB" in pixel font.

### Data Cache
Small boxy unit resembling a RAM stick standing upright in a bracket. Glowing cyan traces along the PCB edge. Noticeably smaller and faster-looking than the database.

### Load Balancer
Office-style network switch box — flat, wide, with a row of blinking ethernet port LEDs across the front. A small digital display shows a rotating arrow icon.

### CDN
Satellite dish or antenna mounted on a small rack shelf. The dish has concentric rings with a cyan glow at the focal point. A coax cable runs down to the base.

### API Gateway
Security checkpoint / badge reader terminal. A small screen shows a lock icon, with a card slot on the side and a green/red indicator light on top. Turnstile or gate aesthetic.

### Queue
Inbox/outbox desk tray — a stackable paper tray with pixel-art documents piling up. The tray has a small LED counter showing queue depth. Papers glow cyan at the edges.

### Worker
Desktop PC workstation — a chunky monitor showing a progress bar, with a keyboard in front. The screen glows cyan. A small "BUSY" indicator light on the monitor bezel.

### Streaming Server
VHS/tape deck unit with reels visibly spinning. A cyan "PLAY" triangle on the front display. Chunky buttons (play/stop/rewind) below the display window.

### Edge Cache
Mini fridge-sized box with a globe/world icon sticker on the front. Faster-looking than CDN — smaller, closer to the user. Antenna nub on top, cyan status ring.

### DNS/GTM
Rotary phone switchboard operator panel — patch cables and jacks with a small directory/address book icon. Cyan lights trace active routes across the patch panel.

### Blob Storage
Filing cabinet — tall, 3-drawer unit with chunky handles. One drawer slightly open showing colorful file folders inside. Label slots on each drawer front glow cyan.

### Circuit Breaker
Literal electrical breaker box mounted on a small wall panel. A large flip switch in the center (up = closed, down = tripped). Red/green indicator light on top, cyan arc graphics when active.
