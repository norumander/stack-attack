# Theme 2: Steampunk / Victorian Mechanical

**Aesthetic:** Brass, copper, and iron machinery powered by steam and clockwork. Every component is a hand-crafted contraption with visible gears, pressure gauges, and steam vents. Ornate but functional — Victorian-era engineers built these to last.

**Palette:** Warm brass/gold, aged copper green (patina), dark iron, mahogany wood accents, amber glass, cyan steam/energy glow for highlights.

**Shared traits:** Every component sits on a riveted iron base plate. Exposed gears, pipes, and gauges are universal. Steam vents release cyan-tinted vapor as the health layer. Brass nameplates label each machine.

---

## Components

### Server
A brass computation engine — Babbage-style difference engine with spinning number wheels behind a glass window. Steam exhaust pipes on top, a large iron crank handle on the side.

### Database
A rotating drum memory vault — large copper cylinder with engraved data grooves, mounted on a mahogany base. A magnifying glass arm reads the surface. Brass lock on the access panel.

### Data Cache
Pneumatic tube station — small brass capsule sits in a glass receiving chamber. Compressed air pipes connect to the sides. Faster delivery = smaller footprint than database.

### Load Balancer
A mechanical railway switch junction — brass levers and track forks mounted on an iron plate. A semaphore signal arm on top indicates current routing. Gears click between positions.

### CDN
Telegraph relay station — brass telegraph key and sounder on a wooden desk. A network of wires fans out from a central pole. The sounder's arm has a cyan electrical arc.

### API Gateway
Iron portcullis gate with a brass ticket window. A mechanical arm stamps "APPROVED" or blocks entry. A pressure gauge shows authentication load. Ornate iron scrollwork frame.

### Queue
Conveyor belt system — a brass and leather belt with small wooden crates queued up single-file. A mechanical counter wheel tracks items. Steam puffs out as each crate advances.

### Worker
A blacksmith's anvil and steam hammer — the hammer arm is mid-strike with cyan sparks flying. A hopper feeds raw materials in from one side, finished products exit the other.

### Streaming Server
A phonograph / gramophone with a large brass horn. The turntable spins continuously. Sound waves (cyan rings) emanate from the horn. An "endless cylinder" feeds the needle.

### Edge Cache
A mechanical pigeon post station — small brass coop with a clockwork pigeon ready to launch. Message capsule loaded. Closer to the user than the telegraph (CDN).

### DNS/GTM
A brass compass and atlas station — a large ornate compass sits on an open map book. The needle spins to find destinations. Brass directional arms point to labeled routes.

### Blob Storage
A massive brass vault/safe — thick riveted door with a combination dial. Oversized compared to other storage — holds the big stuff. Brass reinforcement bands, iron hinges.

### Circuit Breaker
A steam pressure relief valve — large red wheel valve on a pressure pipe. A gauge shows danger levels. When tripped, steam blasts out (cyan vapor). Emergency chain pull beside it.
